Privacy Policy / 隐私政策

Last updated: April 27, 2026

ViralForge helps users create social content, poster copy, and visual assets. This policy explains how data is handled in the app.

ViralForge 用于生成社交平台内容、海报文案与视觉资产。本政策说明 App 如何处理用户数据。

Data We Process / 我们处理的数据

  • Content prompts, product briefs, brand profile fields, generated copy, poster drafts, and app settings.
  • 用户输入的产品简报、品牌资料、生成文案、海报草稿和 App 设置。
  • Subscription status from Apple StoreKit, including product ID and transaction status needed to unlock Pro features.
  • Apple StoreKit 返回的订阅状态，包括用于开通会员功能的商品 ID 与交易状态。

Storage / 存储

Local drafts and settings may be stored on the device. If backend mode is enabled, selected prompts, brand profiles, generated projects, quota state, and subscription sync data may be sent to the configured ViralForge backend.

本地草稿和设置可能保存在设备上。如开启后端模式，部分提示词、品牌资料、生成项目、额度状态与订阅同步数据可能发送到 ViralForge 后端。

AI Providers / AI 服务商

When live generation is enabled, prompts and related generation context may be sent to configured AI providers to produce text or images. API keys are handled on the backend and should not be stored in the iOS app.

启用真实生成时，提示词及相关上下文可能发送给配置的 AI 服务商以生成文本或图片。API Key 由后端管理，不应存储在 iOS App 内。

Photos / 照片

The app asks for permission only when saving generated posters to the photo library.

App 仅在用户保存生成海报到相册时请求照片权限。

Data Deletion / 数据删除

For local-only data, deleting the app removes locally stored drafts and settings from the device. For backend-synced data, email support@viralforge.app with the in-app User ID.

仅保存在本机的数据可通过卸载 App 从设备删除。如数据已同步到后端，请发送邮件至 support@viralforge.app 并附上 App 内用户 ID。

Contact / 联系我们

Email: support@viralforge.app
