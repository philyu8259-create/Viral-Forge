Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 27, 2026

ViralForge is a creation tool for social content, poster copy, and visual assets. This policy explains how the app handles user data.

Data We Process

  • Product briefs, topics, brand profile fields, audience settings, tone settings, banned words, and other creation inputs.
  • Generated copy, poster drafts, project history, favorite status, export status, and app settings.
  • Subscription status from Apple StoreKit, including product ID and transaction status needed to unlock Pro features.

Storage

Local drafts and settings may be stored on the device. If backend mode is enabled, selected prompts, brand profiles, generated projects, quota state, and subscription sync data may be sent to the configured ViralForge backend.

AI Providers

When live generation is enabled, prompts and related generation context may be sent to configured AI providers to produce text or images. API keys are handled on the backend and should not be stored in the iOS app.

Photos

The app asks for permission only when saving generated posters to the photo library.

Data Deletion

For local-only data, deleting the app removes locally stored drafts and settings from the device. For backend-synced data, email philyu2023@qq.com with the in-app User ID.

Contact

Email: philyu2023@qq.com
